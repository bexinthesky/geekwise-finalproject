About

The Mercy Thompson series is written by the wonderful and amazing author Patricia Briggs. She has also written a side series about Charles Cornick, Samuel's brother. It's excellent and explores a completely different side of the inner workings of the werewolf world. There are 10 novels about Mercy Thompson, 3 graphic novels, and a collection of short stories. You should definitely go check them out! To learn more about the author and her work, click the book cover.

The Tri-Cities Area is where most of the Mercy Thompson books take place.
